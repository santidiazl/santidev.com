A 'Pomodoro' clock that allows users to break down work or study sessions into intervals, typically 25 minutes in length followed by 5 minute breaks.
The live demo in link below includes freeCodeCamp's test suite and clock will pass all 29 of its <a href="https://www.freecodecamp.org/learn/front-end-libraries/front-end-libraries-projects/build-a-25--5-clock" target="_blank">required user stories</a>.
